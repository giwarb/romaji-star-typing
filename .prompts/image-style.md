## Pulse Runner Baseline Style

Create crisp browser game sprites for a compact arcade grid game. Use high contrast shapes, readable silhouettes, simple lighting, and a restrained palette that works on dark UI. Keep assets tile-friendly, orthographic, and legible at small sizes. Avoid text inside the asset unless explicitly requested.
